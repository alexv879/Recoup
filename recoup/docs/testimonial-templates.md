## SURVEY QUESTIONS FOR TESTIMONIALS

**Use these 5 targeted questions to collect specific, compelling testimonials:**

### Question 1: The Quantifiable Win (Specificity)
**"How much did Relay help you recover? (¬£ amount)"**
- Why it works: Specific numbers are powerful social proof
- Response format: Short text field with ¬£ prefix
- Expected answer: "¬£2,500" / "¬£8,000"
- Use case: Perfect for pricing page & case studies

---

### Question 2: The Pain Point (Relevance)
**"What problem did Relay solve for you?" (Select one)**
- Why it works: Helps prospects see themselves in your solution
- Response options:
  - Getting paid after a client dispute
  - Protecting my creative work from theft
  - Peace of mind when working with new clients
  - Recovering lost income quickly
  - Confidence to take on bigger projects
- Expected answer: Direct pain point match
- Use case: Landing page testimonials tailored to customer segment

---

### Question 3: The Recommendation (NPS)
**"How likely are you to recommend Relay to other freelancers? (0-10 scale)"**
- Why it works: Classic NPS ‚Äì identifies promoters vs detractors
- Scoring:
  - 9-10 = Promoter (use for testimonials)
  - 7-8 = Passive (follow up to convert)
  - 0-6 = Detractor (qualitative feedback for improvement)
- Use case: Segment for testimonial collection emails

---

### Question 4: The Story (Authenticity)
**"Tell us your Relay story in 1-2 sentences. What changed for you?"**
- Why it works: Opens door to genuine, narrative testimonials
- Response format: Text area (200 character limit)
- Expected answer: "I recovered ¬£3K from a client dispute in 3 days. Game-changer for my freelance business."
- Use case: Landing page, video testimonials, case studies

---
