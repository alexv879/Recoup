## 2. What Are Cookies?

A cookie is a small file of letters and numbers stored on your browser or device. Cookies allow websites to:
- Remember you and your preferences
- Understand how you use the site
- Improve performance and security
- Track usage patterns and analytics

### Types of Cookies:
- **Session Cookies:** Deleted when you close your browser
- **Persistent Cookies:** Remain on your device for a specified period
- **First-Party Cookies:** Set by our domain
- **Third-Party Cookies:** Set by external vendors (analytics, advertising)
