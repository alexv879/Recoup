Service Level Agreement (SLA)

1. Introduction

1.1 Purpose

This Service Level Agreement ("SLA") defines the service levels that Recoup Ltd ("Recoup", "we", "us") commits to provide to customers ("you", "Customer") using our payment automation platform ("Service").

1.2 Scope

This SLA applies to all paid subscription plans. Free trial users and freemium tier users receive service on a best-effort basis without SLA guarantees.

1.3 Incorporation

This SLA is incorporated into and forms part of our Terms of Service. In the event of conflict, the Terms of Service prevail.

2. Service Availability

2.1 Uptime Commitment

Subscription Tier Monthly Uptime Commitment Allowed Downtime/Month
Free/Trial Best effort (no guarantee) N/A
Starter 99.5% ~3.6 hours
Professional 99.9% ~43 minutes
Enterprise 99.95% ~21 minutes

2.2 Uptime Calculation

Uptime percentage is calculated as:

Uptime % = ((Total Minutes in Month - Downtime Minutes) / Total Minutes in Month) Ã— 100

2.3 Exclusions from Uptime Calculation

The following are excluded from downtime calculations:

  • Scheduled maintenance (with at least 48 hours notice)
  • Emergency security patches (reasonable notice given)
  • Issues caused by your actions or configurations
  • Third-party service failures (Stripe, Twilio, SendGrid, Lob, Firebase, Clerk)
  • Force majeure events (natural disasters, war, strikes, etc.)
  • DDoS attacks or other malicious activity
  • Internet backbone failures outside our control
  • Your network or ISP issues
  • Beta features or experimental functionality

2.4 Scheduled Maintenance

We will schedule maintenance windows as follows:

  • Frequency: Maximum once per month
  • Duration: Typically 1-2 hours, maximum 4 hours
  • Timing: During off-peak hours (typically Sunday 22:00-02:00 GMT)
  • Notice: Minimum 48 hours advance notification via email and status page

5. Data Protection and Security

5.1 Data Backup

  • Frequency: Continuous replication with point-in-time recovery
  • Retention: 30 days of backup history
  • Geographic redundancy: Data replicated across multiple UK/EEA regions
  • Recovery Time Objective (RTO): 4 hours for critical data
  • Recovery Point Objective (RPO): Maximum 1 hour of data loss

5.2 Security Measures

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing (minimum annually)
  • Vulnerability scanning and patch management
  • DDoS protection and rate limiting
  • Multi-factor authentication support
  • Role-based access control (RBAC)

5.3 Compliance

  • UK GDPR and Data Protection Act 2018 compliance
  • Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR) compliance
  • PCI DSS compliance (via Stripe for payment processing)
  • Regular compliance audits

6.2 Credit Limitations

  • Maximum credit per month: 50% of monthly subscription fee
  • Maximum credit per year: 3 months of subscription fees
  • Credits applied to future invoices only (no cash refunds)
  • Credits expire if not used within 12 months

6.3 Claiming Credits

To claim service credits:

  1. Submit a request to support@recoup.com within 30 days of the incident
  2. Include:
    • Your account details
    • Dates and times of downtime experienced
    • Description of impact
    • Evidence of downtime (screenshots, error messages)
  3. We will verify the claim within 10 business days
  4. Approved credits applied to your next invoice
Important: Service credits are your sole remedy for SLA breaches. By accepting credits, you waive any other claims for damages related to service availability.
