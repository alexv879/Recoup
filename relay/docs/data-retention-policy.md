## 4. User Deletion Rights

### 4.1 Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten)

Users have the right to request deletion of their account and personal data, subject to legal exceptions.

**How to Request Deletion:**
1. Log into your account and navigate to Account Settings â†’ Delete Account
2. Or email: privacy@[yourdomain].co.uk with subject "Account Deletion Request"
3. Provide proof of identity (government-issued ID)

### 4.2 Deletion Process

Upon receiving a valid deletion request:

1. **Verification (within 5 days):** We verify your identity and confirm account ownership
2. **Preparation (within 10 days):** We prepare data for deletion, excluding legally required records
3. **Deletion (within 30 days):** Personal data is deleted or anonymized
4. **Confirmation (within 30 days):** We send written confirmation of deletion

**Timeline:** Total response time: **30 calendar days from receipt of request**

### 4.3 Exceptions to Deletion

We may retain personal data beyond the user's deletion request if:

- **Tax/Legal Obligation:** Invoices and financial records (7 years for HMRC)
- **Fraud Investigation:** Data related to confirmed fraud (3 years)
- **Active Dispute:** Legal or contractual disputes (until resolved + 2 years)
- **Security Incident:** Data from security breaches (1 year)
- **Legitimate Defense:** Data necessary to defend legal claims
- **Public Record:** Data already in public domain (cannot be deleted)

**We will retain only the minimum necessary data for these exceptions.**

### 4.4 Data Export Before Deletion

Before deleting your account, you can request a complete data export in a portable format:
- **Formats Available:** JSON, CSV, PDF
- **Response Time:** 10 business days
- **Cost:** Free (1x per 12 months; additional exports may incur a fee)

## 6. Data Destruction & Secure Deletion

### 6.1 Secure Deletion Methods

When personal data is deleted, we use:

- **Cryptographic Erasure:** Encryption keys destroyed, rendering data unreadable
- **Overwriting:** Data overwritten with random data (at least 3 passes)
- **Physical Destruction:** Hard drives shredded or incinerated (for backups)

### 6.2 Certification

We maintain records of data destruction and can provide destruction certificates upon request for audit purposes.
