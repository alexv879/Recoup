## COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

| Mistake | Impact | Fix |
|---------|--------|-----|
| Using "noreply@" sender | 40-50% lower opens | Use "Alex from Relay" |
| Adding images to emails | 25-37% lower opens | Remove images, use plain text |
| Multiple CTAs per email | 15-20% lower click | One primary CTA only |
| Long paragraphs (5+ lines) | User skips content | Keep to 3-4 lines max |
| Generic subject lines | Lower open rate | Use curiosity, benefit, or urgency |
| Waiting too long to send Email 2 | Higher drop-off | Send Day 1 if no action by Day 1 |
| Soft upsell on Email 3 | Confusion about free vs paid | Wait until Email 4 for feature intro |
| Too many trigger emails | Unsubscribe spike | Cap at 1 trigger per user per day |
